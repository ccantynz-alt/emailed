# AlecRae v0.1 — The launch

Today we're shipping the first public build of AlecRae. This is the foundation: a complete rewrite of what email should be in 2026.

## New features

- **Unified inbox** across Gmail, Outlook, and any IMAP account
- **AI Compose, Triage, and Reply** powered by Claude
- **Built-in grammar agent** — replaces Grammarly
- **Email-aware dictation** — replaces Dragon
- **Voice profile** that learns how *you* write
- **Bidirectional translation** in 35+ languages
- **True email recall** with link-based revoke
- **Snooze, schedule send, and undo send**
- **End-to-end encryption** (RSA-OAEP-4096 + AES-256-GCM)
- **Local-first IndexedDB cache** — sub-100ms inbox loads
- **Full keyboard control** with a Cmd+K command palette
- **Public REST + tRPC API** with webhooks

## Pricing

AlecRae launches with five plans: Free, Personal ($9), Pro ($19), Team ($12/user), and Enterprise.

## Breaking changes

This is the first release. Nothing to break yet. Future breaking changes will be called out here loudly and given a deprecation window.
